Baha'is believe that children are the most precious treasure a community can possess and thus place emphasis on their spiritual and moral development. Classes for the spiritual education of children are organized at the neighborhood level.

Children's classes are ongoing in a variety of locations, mostly in lower Westchester. Please email atc@westchesterbahais.org with your name and township to be directed to the nearest neightborhood classes.
